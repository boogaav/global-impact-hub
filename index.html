01

Vision

A network of innovation and development hubs across Vietnam — combining the strengths of a social enterprise, community hub, research center, startup incubator, and the arts & creative sector under one roof.

02

Why now

Vietnam is entering a new era shaped by artificial intelligence, digital transformation, climate change, demographic shifts, and global economic restructuring. Innovation ecosystems are growing — but significant gaps remain between research, entrepreneurship, community needs, and international collaboration.

Universities struggle to translate research into impact. Startups lack access to networks and evidence-based support. Communities remain disconnected from innovation opportunities. A new model is needed — one that brings knowledge creation, community building, and venture development together.

Core functions

a

Social Enterprise Platform

Sustainable revenue through executive education, research & consulting, innovation retreats, co-working spaces, and international fellowships — reinvested into community programs, scholarships, research, and startup support.

b

Research & Policy Hub

Applied research on AI and digital transformation, entrepreneurship, sustainable development, future skills, governance, climate resilience, and community development — publications, policy briefs, datasets, and open-access knowledge.

c

Community Hub

An inclusive space for students, entrepreneurs, researchers, local communities, and international experts — public lectures, networking events, mentorship, community projects, and innovation challenges.

d

Innovation & Incubation Hub

Support for early-stage ventures and social enterprises — mentorship, investor access, pilot testing, market intelligence, and international exposure, focused on ventures creating both economic and social value.
